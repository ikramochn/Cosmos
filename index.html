SPACE? ASTRONOMY?

     

Space and astronomy captivate with their exploration of
celestial entities, events, and the boundless universe beyond
our planet. Space denotes the expansive realm beyond Earth's
atmosphere, encompassing planets, stars, galaxies, and other
cosmic bodies. Astronomy, in turn, is the disciplined inquiry into
these celestial objects and occurrences. Fundamental elements
of astronomy comprise planets, stars, nebulae, galaxies, and
the enigmatic phenomena of black holes.    

SOLAR SYSTEM

 

PLANETS

Planets orbit the Sun, divided into rocky and gas types. Terrestrial planets are rocky, while gas giants have massive atmospheres..

STARS

Stars fall into two groups: terrestrial and giant planets. Terrestrial ones are rocky with thin atmospheres, while giants have massive gas atmospheres..

GALAXIES

Galaxies are immense collections of stars, solar systems, gas, and dust held together by gravity. The Milky Way, our home galaxy, is just one among billions..

BLACK HOLE

A black hole is a cosmic object formed from the collapse of massive stars, with an intense gravitational pull that nothing, not even light, can escape..

NEBULAE

Nebulae are vast clouds of gas and dust vital to stars' life cycles, classified into emission, reflection, planetary, and dark types..
